Two - On ownership - 2020-12-29

I spent most of my time so far working for companies that cater to multiple customers. At the beginning of my career it was slicing and coding basic websites with the optional contact form, sometimes multiple a week, sometimes even more than one a day! Can you imagine? 4 hours between receiving a .psd file and draggin the whole folder into filezilla and hoping for the best... Typically I worked on these projects alone. Years later we're talking projects that take multiple months or sometimes years to complete. Typically with 2-4 devs and a project manager.

They are usually not projects but products in disguise. Or even worse, products treated and budgetted as projects.

And there is one big reason for this mess: The company that codes it, does not own it.
